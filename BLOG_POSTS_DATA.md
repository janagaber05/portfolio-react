### Column: `slug` â­ IMPORTANT
**What to write:** URL-friendly version of the title

**Examples:**

**Format:**
- Lowercase letters only
- Use hyphens `-` instead of spaces
- No special characters
- Should be unique (one slug per blog post)
- Used in URL: `/blogs/designing-with-emotion...`

**How to create slug:**
1. Take the title
2. Convert to lowercase
3. Replace spaces with hyphens
4. Remove special characters
5. Keep it short but descriptive

## ğŸ’¡ Tips

1. **Slug:**
   - Make it unique and descriptive
   - Use lowercase and hyphens
   - Keep it relatively short (50-100 characters)

2. **Title:**
   - Make it catchy and clear

6. **Published:**
   - Set to `false` while writing
   - Change to `true` when ready to publish
